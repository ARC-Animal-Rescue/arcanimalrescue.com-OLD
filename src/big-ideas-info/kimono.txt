KIMONO PROJECT: Following the creation of an art project that interprets a specific Kimono as the art project develops over a year.  Each month, a celebrity is photographed wearing the Kimono as the celebrity autograph the current iteration of the developing art project.  At the end of the project, the Kimono and art project are auctioned off together to benefit the Rescue.
