PET PHOTO PARTNERSHIP:  This nationwide partnership agreement between shelters and Photo organizations, professionals, colleges, high schools, Clubs, shows off the best side of pets in shelters!  One afternoon a week, photographers bring their skill set to create dazzling , adoption-generating photos of new arrivals at the shelter.  Creative backgrounds, dozens of great photos and videos, edited with funny memes,  feature the adorableness that makes  families create a waiting list to adopt!
